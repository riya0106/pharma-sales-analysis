# Exploratory Data Analysis of Pharmaceutical Sales

## Overview

This project analyzes daily pharmaceutical sales data from 2014–2019 across eight ATC drug categories. The goal was to identify category performance, yearly trends, seasonal patterns, weekday demand, and relationships between drug categories.

## Business Questions

* Which drug categories dominate overall sales?
* How do sales trends change over time?
* Are there seasonal or weekday demand patterns?
* Which categories move together?

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Date/time feature creation
* Monthly and yearly sales aggregation
* Category-level sales comparison
* Seasonal and weekday trend analysis
* Correlation analysis between drug categories

## Key Findings

* N02BE (Analgesic Combinations) contributed the largest share of total sales.
* Sales were generally higher during winter months.
* Saturdays showed the highest average sales activity.
* Most drug categories had independent demand patterns.
* 2019 sales appeared lower because the dataset only includes data through October.

## Business Impact

This analysis can help support inventory planning, demand forecasting, staff scheduling, and seasonal sales preparation.
